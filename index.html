Introduction

My primary research focuses include applied machine learning and its applications in computer vision, computational chemistry, computational physics, cybersecurity, and exobiology. Currently, I concentrate on using graph neural networks to predicate molecule properties, and on applying self-supervised learning to study protein folding paths.

Despite my busy teaching schedule, I have published over twenty papers in journals and international conference proceedings, including ACM Multimedia Conference, IEEE Trans. on Multimedia, ACS Journal of Physical Chemistry(b), ACS LANGMUIR, Journal of Chemical Theory and Computation, IEEE International Conference on Machine Learning and Applications, ACM Trans. on Multimedia Computing, Communications and Applications.
